# Task: Career Intelligence SaaS - Product Definition

## Objective
Define MVP for Oliver's Career Intelligence SaaS based on his proprietary system.

## Oliver's System (Reverse-Engineer from Context)
Based on his Google PM role + ex-Microsoft background + agentic dev work, likely includes:
- Job search tracking & management

- Career progression planning
- Skill gap analysis

- Network mapping

## MVP Feature Prioritization

### Must-Have (Launch Blockers)
1. **Job Tracker**
   - Save jobs from any source
   - Track application status
   - Notes & reminders
   - Browser extension for 1-click save

2. **Salary Intelligence**
   - Compensation benchmarking (levels.fyi data)
   - Negotiation scripts
   - Offer comparison calculator

3. **AI Career Coach**
   - Resume feedback
   - Cover letter generation
   - Interview prep questions
   - Career advice chat

### Nice-to-Have (Post-Launch)
4. **Interview Scheduler**
   - Calendar integration
   - Prep reminders
   - Post-interview follow-up templates

5. **Network Map**
   - LinkedIn import
   - Referral tracking
   - Connection suggestions

6. **Analytics Dashboard**
   - Application funnel
   - Success metrics
   - Time-to-hire tracking

### Future (Monetization Opportunities)
7. **Premium Coaching**
   - 1:1 sessions with Oliver
   - Mock interviews
   - Negotiation support

8. **Company Intelligence**
   - Culture fit scores
   - Team insights
   - Growth trajectory

## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Tier (Free)
- Track up to 10 jobs
- Basic salary data
- Limited AI coach usage (10 messages/month)

### Pro Tier ($49/month or $399/year)
- Unlimited job tracking
- Full salary intelligence
- Unlimited AI coach
- Browser extension
- Priority support

### Enterprise Tier ($199/month)
- Everything in Pro
- 1 coaching session/month with Oliver
- Custom integrations
- Team features (for bootcamps/universities)

## Target Customer

### Primary: Mid-Career Tech Workers
- Software engineers, PMs, designers
- 3-10 years experience
- Actively job searching or passively looking
- Earning $100k-$300k
- Want to optimize their career moves

### Secondary: Career Switchers
- Bootcamp grads
- Transitioning to tech
- Need structured guidance

### Competitors
- **Teal:** Job tracking + resume builder

- **Blind:** Anonymous career discussions
- **ChatGPT:** Generic career advice

### Oliver's Differentiators
1. **Integrated intelligence:** All tools in one place
2. **AI coach trained on tech careers:** Not generic advice
3. **Salary negotiation focus:** Based on real Google/MSFT experience
4. **Product-led growth:** Free tier is genuinely useful
