# 90-Day Career Change Blueprint

## Your Week-by-Week Roadmap to a New Career

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

### Week 1: Define Your Target

**Day 1-2: Self-Assessment**
- [ ] List your top 5 transferable skills
- [ ] Identify 3 industries that interest you
- [ ] Research 10 target roles (job descriptions)

**Day 3-4: Market Research**
- [ ] Analyze 20 job postings in target role
- [ ] Extract common requirements
- [ ] Identify skill gaps

**Day 5-7: Define Your Goal**
- [ ] Write one-sentence career goal
- [ ] Identify target company types (startup/mid/enterprise)
- [ ] Set success criteria (salary, title, industry)

**Output:** Career Change Canvas (clear target)

---

### Week 2: Learning Plan

- [ ] Identify 3-5 core skills to learn
- [ ] Find best resources (courses, books, communities)
- [ ] Set up portfolio website (GitHub Pages/Carrd)
- [ ] Refresh LinkedIn profile
- [ ] Write intro post: "I'm changing careers..."

**Output:** Learning roadmap + public presence

---

## Phase 2: Build (Week 3-8)

### Week 3-5: Project 1 (Foundation)

**Goal:** Prove you can do basic work

- [ ] Choose project that solves real problem
- [ ] Build and ship (even if imperfect)
- [ ] Document process in blog post
- [ ] Add to portfolio
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn

**Time investment:** 20-30 hours

---

### Week 6-8: Project 2 (Depth)

**Goal:** Prove you understand nuance

- [ ] More ambitious project
- [ ] Involves research/analysis
- [ ] Create detailed case study
- [ ] Share publicly
- [ ] Get feedback from practitioners

**Time investment:** 30-40 hours

---

## Phase 3: Network (Week 7-10)

### Week 7: Research

- [ ] Create list of 50 people in target role
- [ ] Prioritize by: relevance, accessibility, active online
- [ ] Track in spreadsheet

---

### Week 8-9: Engage

- [ ] Follow all 50 people
- [ ] Engage on their content (thoughtful comments)
- [ ] Share their work with your insights
- [ ] Join relevant communities (Slack/Discord)

**Goal:** Get on their radar (10-15 people)

---

### Week 10: Outreach

- [ ] DM 10-15 people for quick feedback
- [ ] Offer value first (not "pick your brain")
- [ ] Schedule 5+ informational conversations
- [ ] Ask about their journey, advice
- [ ] Build genuine relationships

**Output:** 5 warm connections

---

## Phase 4: Apply (Week 10-14)

### Week 10-11: Target Companies

- [ ] Create tiered list (5 dream, 10 fit, 5 backup)
- [ ] Research each deeply (product, team, problems)
- [ ] Identify connections at each
- [ ] Prepare custom assets

---

### Week 12-13: Applications

**Tier 1 (5 companies):**
- [ ] Deep customization (analyze their product)
- [ ] Create "90-day plan" doc
- [ ] Direct outreach to hiring manager
- [ ] Include portfolio prominently

**Tier 2 (10 companies):**
- [ ] Customized resume/cover letter
- [ ] Reference specific problems
- [ ] Ask for referral (if connection)

**Tier 3 (5 companies):**
- [ ] Quality application
- [ ] Portfolio linked

---

### Week 14: Follow-up

- [ ] Follow up on all applications
- [ ] Continue networking
- [ ] Keep building/sharing content

---

## Phase 5: Interview (Week 12-16)

### Interview Prep

- [ ] Practice career change narrative
- [ ] Prepare portfolio walk-through
- [ ] Research each company deeply
- [ ] Prepare questions to ask

**The Narrative:**
1. Your relevant background
2. What you discovered
3. What you did (portfolio)
4. Why this field
5. How your background helps

---

### Interview Performance

- [ ] Confidently explain transition
- [ ] Show portfolio prominently
- [ ] Handle "lack of experience" objection
- [ ] Demonstrate deep research
- [ ] Follow up within 24 hours

## Time Investment

**Weekly breakdown:**
- Learning: 5-10 hours
- Building: 10-15 hours
- Networking: 3-5 hours
- Applying: 5-10 hours (later weeks)

**Total: 20-30 hours/week**

---

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1: Skipping networking
❌ Just applying cold
✅ Network while building

### Pitfall 2: Perfect project syndrome
❌ Spending 3 months on one project
✅ Ship in 2-3 weeks, iterate

### Pitfall 3: Not documenting publicly
❌ Building in silence
✅ Share progress weekly

### Pitfall 4: Waiting too long to apply
❌ "I'm not ready yet" (Week 20)
✅ Start applying Week 10-12
