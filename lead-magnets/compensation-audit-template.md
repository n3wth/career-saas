# Compensation Audit Template
## Find Money You're Losing (And Fix It)

Use this template to audit your compensation gaps and identify opportunities to improve retention.

## Part 1: Internal Compensation Audit

### Setup
1. Export your HRIS data: Role, Tenure, Performance Rating, Salary, Location
2. Remove PII from names (keep employee IDs only)
3. Create columns for analysis

### Quick Analysis Questions

1. **Are high performers underpaid?**
   - Filter: Performance 4-5 rating AND Salary < Market - $10K
   - Result: These people are leaving. Fix immediately.

2. **Is there salary compression?**
   - Compare: Junior vs Mid vs Senior salaries
   - Healthy: 15-25% spread between levels
   - Unhealthy: < 10% spread (people feel undifferentiated)

3. **Are tenure/experience reflected in pay?**
   - Compare: Year 1-2 employees vs 5+ year employees
   - Healthy: 5-10% raises per year
   - Unhealthy: No correlation to tenure (demoralizing)

4. **Is there gender/demographic salary gap?**
   - Compare: Same role, similar performance
   - Target: <5% variance (anything else is a problem)

5. **Who's at retention risk?**
   - Filter: Salary < Market - $15K
   - Flag: These will get recruited by competitors

## Part 3: Retention Risk Scoring

### Risk Factors (Score 1-5)

| Factor | Description | Your Score |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Compensation** | Salary gap vs market | _/5 |
| **Performance** | Recent review score | _/5 |
| **Tenure** | Years with company (5+ year employees higher risk) | _/5 |
| **Growth** | Promotion timeline (2+ years stuck = high risk) | _/5 |
| **External Demand** | Role market competitiveness | _/5 |

**Score calculation:** (Compensation + Performance + Tenure + Growth + External Demand) / 5

**Interpretation:**
- **4-5:** HIGH RISK - Likely to be recruited soon
- **3-3.9:** MEDIUM RISK - Vulnerable if competitor approaches
- **2-2.9:** LOW RISK - Likely to stay
- **<2:** VERY LOW RISK - Locked in

### Action by Risk Level

**HIGH RISK (4-5):**
- Have career conversation THIS WEEK
- Offer market-rate compensation adjustment
- Discuss growth path and next promotion
- Understand what they want next
- Consider retention bonus if deserved

**MEDIUM RISK (3-3.9):**
- Schedule coffee chat (quarterly)
- Understand career goals
- Create development plan
- Monitor market feedback
- Prepare compensation adjustment if needed

**LOW RISK (2-2.9):**
- Regular 1:1 check-ins
- Quarterly career conversations
- Annual benchmarking

**VERY LOW RISK (<2):**
- Focus on career development
- Annual check-ins
